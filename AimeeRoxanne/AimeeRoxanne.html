My name is Aimee Roxanne.

Born and raised in CO, I grew up in the burbs of Boco. I was a jock for the first half of my life but that feels VERY foreign to me now. These days I much prefer sitting still and entertainment like puzzles and podcasts.

 

Denver > Boulder

I’ve lived in Denver for the last 10 years. Until recently, I was totally that girl who said “Ugh I could never live in Boulder again,” with authentic disdain. Things change though, and Denver has too. I’ve been back in Boco for about 6 months and I have to say, I really dig how quiet it is here. I also don’t hate getting to see the stars on a regular basis and not feeling like I’m going to die every time I get on my bike.
