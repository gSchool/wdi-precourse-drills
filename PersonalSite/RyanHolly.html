About Me

I grew up in Phoenix, AZ, and went to undergrad at the University of Denver, where I studied Biology. I was a member of the NCAA D1 swimming team, and I swam butterfly and freestyle. After DU, I went to UC Berkeley to pursue my PhD in Biology. I realized early on in my PhD that biology research wasn't for me, and decided to change careers. So, I moved back to Denver, got married, and decided to become a web developer. I'm excited to start Galvanize!
