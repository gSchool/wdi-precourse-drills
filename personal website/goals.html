It's definitely been a while since I've taken those classes and things have changed a bit in the coding world. I have been enjoying getting my feet wet again and look forward to learning everything that I can.

It was fun for a while, but it was not challenging, and after missing so many holidays with my family I decided it was time to start over.

I am looking forward to beginning a new career after Galvanize that actually involves something that I enjoy and that will continue to challenge me.
