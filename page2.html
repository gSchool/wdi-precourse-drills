My Life's Direction

I'm stoked to be around like-minded people and for the opportunity to learn meaningful things that will help me progress in the future. I don't know who I will become; however, I have a pretty good idea that it'll be someone who has a farm and lives somewhere cold. After having been fairly stagnant over the past couple years, I'm ready to get back up and start making a difference again. I don't have a specific company in mind that I'd like to work for. I just want to be able to use my brain on a daily basis for meaningful tasks.

I want to eventually have a farm and would love to have that become a rehabilitation facility for people suffering from addiction (or just suffering from life.) That would be my ultimate goal in life in terms of helping others and making a difference.
