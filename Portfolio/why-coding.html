I obtained a B.A. of Psychology from CU Boulder in 2014 and then went into management in the medical cannabis industry. I learned a lot about cannabis as alternative medicine and had many opportunities to grow as an individual and help others who had exhausted all other options. However, I ultimately sought out web development hoping to find more of a challenge and build a career with more long-term stability and room for growth. I am passionate about all things tech related, so making the jump into web development is, I hope, the first step on a long journey of education, exploration, and self-discovery.
