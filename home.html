About Me



Hello! My name is Jacob Crane. I was born December 1st, 1989 in Brandon, Florida. My father was working for my grandfather on a construction job down there. My parents moved to Colorado soon after and I grew up in Aurora.

Growing up I did all kinds of activities. I took Taekwondo lessons, played sports, acted in plays, skateboarded, and even tried to start a band. Once I got to High School, my friends convinced me to play Lacrosse. I was hooked on the sport and immediately did everything I could to get my grades up and get better at Lacrosse in order to play in college. I played at two different schools and coached at my High School after college.

Since college, I have been a personal trainer at 24 Hour Fitness. My clients were amazing and many have become my closest friends. One in particular introduced me to coding. Since his help, I have decided to further advance my skills in computer programming. You can read more about my aspirations here .

As far as my social life, I enjoy many different things in my down time. Most of all, I enjoy time with my beautiful wife, Ashley! We love to hike, try new foods, go on dates and work on our house. I also have a group of frineds that plays Magic: The Gathering roughly twice a month.
