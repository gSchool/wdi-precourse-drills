A Short Story: Chapter 1

An Introduction

My name is Paige and I grew up in Albuqueurque, New Mexico. I moved to Austin almost 6 years ago but before that I lived in Fort Worth, Boulder and Rome, Italy. I went to school at St. Edwards University and double majored in Art History and English Literature. Since graduation I have worked at a couple differnt start-ups across Austin. I started off at Favor where I worked as a Content Quality Assistant and from there moved over to a company called OwnLocal. At OwnLocal I worked as an Account Support Representative helping Newspapers digitalize their local ads in order to help create an online presence for small businesses. My most recent position was at TrendKite a PR Analytics company. Using boolean logic I built out searches for various companies that powered dashboards that could report on brands presence in the media.

During my time at OwnLocal and TrendKite I fell in the love with the technology industry and came to realize that this was where I wanted to build my career!
