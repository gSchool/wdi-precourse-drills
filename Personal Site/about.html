"One person can make a difference, and everyone should try"

- John F. Kennedy

As I’ve explored my various passions in my life, from knitting scarves to lifting weights, from tutoring classmates to starting my own business, I always find something to learn from each new experience. I quickly discovered that I would learn most when I am giving it my all. It is for this exact reason I want to start my new career in computer programming; to continue my personal growth and understanding of the world in which we live in order to improve it.

Work Hard Play Harder.

Looking back, I now understand that always doing my best has indirectly given me leadership experience throughout my life. My various mentors have each shared a life’s worth of knowledge and experience, so I’ve always strived to give back by teaching others what I have learned from my own experiences. I believe learning computer programming will allow me to build a platform for the rest of my life, not only preparing me for a successful career but also to make a true impact in the world.

Chase your dreams, Manifest your own destiny.

Everyone was put on this world to make a difference – it is up to the individual whether or not he or she chooses to live his or her life in such a way. My goal in life is to provide inspiration for others to understand we all have what it takes to make a difference in the world. All it takes is a little hard work and perseverance.

So, what are you waiting for?
