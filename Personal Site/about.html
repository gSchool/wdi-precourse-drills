About Me

What up nerds, I'm Nancy. I'm 25 and I've spent most my life in the Northwest: I grew up in Washington, and went to college in Oregon. I love the PNW, but I'm looking forward to spending the winter in Phoenix.

I graduated with a bachelor's in Economics from Oregon State in '16, and I've been working in as an analyst in an environmental testing lab since then. It's not very exciting.

I spend most of my free time at the gym: I'm passionate about health and fitness, I love strength training, and I'm always learning more about the nutrition side. I also enjoy cooking when it fits with my diet plan, hiking while the weather's nice, reading comfy fantasy, and messing with my aquariums. You can check out some of my photographic proof below.
