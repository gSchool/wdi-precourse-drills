A Bit About Me

Background

I grew up here in the valley. I was born at St. Luke's hospital in Tempe, AZ and have lived in Tempe and the surrounding cities since. After high school, I attended Arizona State University for a few years before transferring to Univeristy of Phoenix where I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Management. For about 5 years, I worked in the insurance industry in a variety of roles. Recently I made the decision that I would like to pursue a career in web development and so I left the company and am now beginning my new path here at Galvanize!
