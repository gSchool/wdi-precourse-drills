In The Future

I am excited...I am terrified...I am excited again.

These emotions have been cycling through my body ever since I decided to go through Galvanize's Immersive Web Developement program. I am sure these emotions will continue throughout the course, along with many others.

Truely, I am more excited than anything else! Because, I have goals...big goals. I would love to continue learning and building on what Galvanize teaches me through working with a team of programmers in building applications such as REI's Hiking Project or Andy Puddicombe's Headspace. I am a musician at heart and where I would like to see myself in 10 years is building online and offline tools for musicians. Tools such as software and hardware similar to Ableton Live and Monome.
