A Little Snippit About Me

Hi there! My name is Alexis, but I go by Ali or Tia to my brother's kiddos. I've lived in Phoenix almost my entire life, but get out & travel as often as possible. When I'm not hanging out with my dog, I like to dabble in all kinds of projects ranging from woodwork to sewing to painting.

Family

I'm so lucky to belong to these people! My parents are incredibly supportive & are playing a huge role in allowing me to reinvent myself once again. My older brother & his wife have two amazing kiddos & one on the way.

Dog Mom

Meet my dog, Pesto. He's a 10-month-young lab-mix. We love to hike and travel together. He's a very sweet baby pony weighing in at 58 lbs. and growing.

Vagabond

I've been incredibly fortunate to travel a ton over the years. My most recent walkabout took Pesto & I on a roadtrip around the Southwest over about 6 weeks.
