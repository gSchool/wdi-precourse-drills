My Goals

I'm excited, and also a bit nervous, about starting Galvanize. I chose Galvanize because my friends had such great experiences here, and I like Galvanize's class times and schedule. I believe I will be able to retain more information from Galvanize than I could at a bootcamp with 12-13 hour days and, therefore, will get more out of this program. I also like that being a software developer can potentially allow me to travel more often, especially if I find a job where I can work remotely.

My background

My first experience with coding was when I was a teenager. I liked to play with virtual paper dolls called KiSS dolls and eventually started making them myself. Part of this process involves coding your doll and all its clothes into the KiSS program. I found that I enjoyed the coding part of doll-making more than the drawing part. When the largest archive of dolls on the internet became a pay site I decided to teach myself HTML so I could build websites to host my dolls. My site background is a nod to that experience because I discovered KiSS dolls while trying to find Sailor Moon information online. You can see a screenshot of one of my old dolls here. I've been taking classes towards a Computer Science degree at ACC for the last two and a half years, but I got burned out. I still have the same career goals, and Galvanize seems like a much better way to achieve them.
