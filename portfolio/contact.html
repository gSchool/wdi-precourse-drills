Who am I? Where am I from?

I have a long personal history, many facts, stories, roads travelled, mountains climbed. In the stories, I am both hero and villain, and when the play is over and we take off our masks, who will we be?

Behind the mask, I am a spiritual being of mysterious origin, living in physical world, a paradoxical world with many problems, yet it is a world that is perfect just the way it is.

What do I hope to achieve? I want to live in a state of child-like love. Do you know how a child naturally has a love for all living things? Like that.

And in the spirit of child-like love, I’d like to do some good actions while I’m here. In this case, I want to build web applications that help people get stuff done. I want to work together on teams, with creativity, enthusiasm, and respect for diversity.
