AmeriCorps

The reason why I am Denver is because of a national service program called AmeriCorps NCCC. In 2008, I was stationed in Denver and, for two years, traveled around the country and was involved with several national service projects.

I was a wild land firefighter in Missouri and in Colorado, built houses in New Orleans for Hurricane Katrina victims, and many others! When AmeriCorps was over, I liked Denver so much, I decided to stay!

Non-Profit

Although I am changing my career, my career ultimate goal is end up in the same field of focus. I would love to build technology for renewable energy or environmental sustainability companies.
