M A T T H E W // H Y R K A

I was born and raised in TN. I attended the University of Tennessee from 2006-2010 (Go Vols) and graduated with a degree in Political Science. After graduation I decided to take my band on tour. We played over a hundred shows a year and were featured at Bonnaroo, Hangout, Voodoo, Summer Camp, and myriad other smaller festivals. The music business is a cruel mistress so despite some success and encouraging progress we eventually decided that touring was no longer what we wanted to do and went on hiatus. I moved to Denver last October and excited for the next chapter in my life.
