About Me

A few little details...

A few years from now, I hope to be a full stack developer for a company that encourages innovation, that fosters creativity, and that will help me grow as a person and as a young professional. I also wish to take the skills I gain at Galvanize and use them to develop a youth education program that teaches and encourages kids to explore coding and computer science.

I'm an expert on How Not To Get Trampled By An Elephant. When I'm not working, I'm usually either reading or exploring the city. I'm a neurotic minimalist; I can fit all of my belongings in a truck, and this includes furnishings. I always have the key code to Red Headed Step Child, so give me a shout-out if you want it. I was born in Texas and have no intention of ever leaving Austin, not unless it's to see my partner, who lives halfway across the world in Botswana (pictured below).
