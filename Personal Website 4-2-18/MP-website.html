Melissa & Family!




Hi there! My name is Melissa and I go by Melissa. I was born and raised in Chicago and have spent all my life there, until recently...My partner, Chuck and I decided change sceneries from sky skapper buildings to a mountain backdrop. We now live in Jamestown, Colorado (which is about 8 miles from North Boulder). Our 3 year old daughter and 5 year old french bulldog couldn't have been any happier with this move, they now get to enjoy the outdoors all year round.

As a family we spend a lot of time outside, and individually we are always looking for our next adventure. Chuck and I are both ultra runners (that is how we met), and are often training for trail race. I won't be racing much this year, but I have my eye on this race next May in Goshen, VA called Infinitus 888k (all running). It's 27 mile loops in a figure 8 shape. This race is pretty much self supported on the front end, so you need to be self reliant. It has a 10% finishers rate and so far only two men have finished and no female finishers (yet). I attempted it last year and fell short but averaged a marathon a day over the 10 days of the race. I learned a lot from that experience and I want to give it another go.
