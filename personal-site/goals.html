Goals

As a self-taught graphic and web designer, I've always been fascinated with the development aspect of building websites – I simply haven't had a chance to pursue it beyond the scope of basic JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP. With Galvanize, I hope to finally receive formal training on both front-end and back-end development and be able to build beautiful and functional websites on my own. After Galvanize, I would love to work for a company that will allow me to not only build websites, but also make use of my design chops too!
