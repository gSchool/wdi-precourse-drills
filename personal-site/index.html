Personal Site of Ryan Wittrup

About Me:

My name is Ryan Wittrup and I am an aspiring web developer and backend engineer.

I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio and went to the University of Dayton where I majored in chemical engineering.

I first was introduced to programming and coding in a freshman workshop where we used Lego Mindstorms to build cars which would use a vision system to follow a small course.

Hobbies:

In my freetime I try to stay active. I ski in the winters and play volleyball and softball once spring and summer come.

I also go to the gym and practice yoga and meditation several times a week - it's always good to just step back and take a breath sometimes.

One new skill I'm trying to learn is drawing and painting. I have a long ways to go, but I especially would like to get in to watercolors and urban sketching - I love how raw and intimate it can be to just put to paper something ordinary but in an extraordinary way.

Family and Friends:

My family is relatively small, and almost everybody is still in the Cleveland area.

I'm an only child (though not too socially awkward) and my parents live in a suburb about 20 minutes from the city.

I'm also fortunate to have friends who have gone on to live all around the country. It means a few trips each year to Denver, Chicago, Seattle, and Las Vegas which are always a good time.
