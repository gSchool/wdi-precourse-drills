Why web development?

I started dabbling in web development in 2012 after a friend of mine convinced me to tryout Javascript. I have become fascinated by code and the web. I love that you are not limited by physical building materials, but only by time, the skills you have, and fucks given.

A professional dancer

I began dancing when I was 10. I disliked it at first. But, do to supportive parents, a kick ass teacher, and being stubborn, I fell in love with dancing and performing.

I enjoyed 7 years as a professional dancer. I have danced all over North America with a number of companies including - Houston Ballet, Oregon Ballet Theatre, Tulsa Ballet, Rochester City Ballet and Alberta Ballet. I have Performed works by - Jiří Kylián, George Balanchine, Ben Stevenson, John Cranko, Ronald Hynd, Twyla Tharp, Stanton Welch, James Kudelka, Aszure Barton and Wen Wei Wang.

I am looking forward to starting a career in web development and learning how to dance with my brain!

Web Projects

Personal site

This personal website and its different style options (listed bellow) were inspired by the CSS Zen Garden by Dave Shea. The idea was to showcase the diversity of CSS by having people submit different style sheets that expressed different layouts for a single unchanging HTML document . I wanted to recreate this idea for myself to better understand the relashionship between HTML and CSS.
