About Me

Hi! My name's Chris and I was born in Austin. I've got a few years of coding experience, but I decided to dive into Galvanize to really go deep into web development. For fun I do theatre! Most of my scripted performances are at The Curtain Theatre with The Baron's Men. For improv, I've done most of my work at The Hideout and am in the troupes Too Shy Guys and Human People.
