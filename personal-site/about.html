Welcome

Hi there! My name is Ed. I was born Vermont and spent my my first 19 years of life exploring the green mountains in the north east. In my youth, I enjoyed playing soccer, lacrosse, camping, and water sports. At age 19 I moved to Colorado for college where I spent 3 years at CU studying human physiology before becoming employed at a startup. The company, eGauge Systems, manufactures smart meters for measuring electricity (egauge.net). After four years working in sales and digital marketing, I went back to CU and concurrently completed a degree in human physiology. Almost exactly a year later, here we are...

I was introduced to web development at eGauge where I was in charge of designed and implimenting much of the company website, along with other marketing related tasks. When I was bored, or wasn't sure what to work on, I improved the features and user experince of our website. Finally, I decided to commit to learning the craft formally.

How I feel about starting Galvanize...

I've met several web developers throughout my professional career who graduated from a dev bootcamp and highly recommended it. Those who I worked with directly often suggested that I should take one because of my experience and interest. I see Galvanize immersive as a way to become qualified for a new career very quickly.
