Keith Decker

Hello! My name is Keith Decker. I am originally from Maryland but I call Denver home now. I spent the last 6 years working in the craft beer industry and now I am very excited to learn more about web development!

Maryland

I was born in Silver Spring, Maryland which is right outside of Washington DC. My family moved to Howard County, Maryland when I was in the second grade and that is where I spent the rest of my childhood. I spent a lot of time on the water growing up. We lived right next to the Triadelphia reservoir and had a garage full of kayaks and canoes. I also have fond memories of catching blue crabs and fishing for striped bass on the Chesapeake Bay. After graduating from high school I attended college in West Virgina at Shepherd University where I studed business and sociology

Colorado

The summer before my senior year in College a friend of mine and I decided to road trip across the country and see what the West had to offer. The first time I saw the Rocky Mountains I fell in love. I moved to Denver immediately after graduation in 2009 and haven't looked back. I had just arrived in Denver and was hungry to meet new people. I found community in the rapidly growing craft beer industry. I started spening more time immersing myself in that culture and eventually took a job at Renegade Brewing Company working in the taproom and helping with the sales program. I spent the following 6 years as the Sales Manager there and that is where I met my lovely wife Laura. We now live in the Baker neighborhood with three cute cats named Slate, Cinder, and Pepper.
