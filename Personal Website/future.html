Future Plans:

Why Galvanize?

There aren't a lot of news/media jobs in Denver, so I knew I had to rethink my career plans before we moved here. A friend who went to a bootcamp in San Francisco suggested looking programs here, and that's how I wound up at Galvanize.

I don't come from a tech background and it's never been something that I've had a burning desire to get into. The appeal for me is that I like to learn and I want to want to do something that will continue to be interesting down the road.

Goals

I'm not focused on getting any particular type of web development job, largely because I don't know enough to make a decision like that right now. I'm hoping there'll be something I can develop some facility in, or parts of the course that stand out to me.

Aspirations

  1. To have a balanced life and not work 60 hours a week like I used to.
  2. Find a job where I enjoy going to work every day
  3. Get a pet or two.
