WHERE I'M HEADED

With Galvanize, I plan to learn as much as I can about how the internet grows so rapidly, and the processes people use to build the most interesting websites.

After Galvanize, I plan on finding work in Colorado as a web developer while pursuing other new languages in my free time. Someday, I'd love to be creating video games that others can enjoy.
